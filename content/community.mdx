# Community

Join the SupaSidebar community to connect with other users, share feedback, and help shape the future of the app.

## Join Discord

Our Discord server is the best place to:

- **Discuss features** - Talk about how you use SupaSidebar and share tips
- **Request features** - Tell us what you'd like to see next
- **Report issues** - Get help from the community and the developer
- **Stay updated** - Be the first to know about new releases and updates

## Request a Feature

Have an idea for SupaSidebar? We'd love to hear it! Here's how:

1. **Join our Discord** using the link above
2. Head to the feature requests channel
3. Describe your idea and how it would help your workflow
4. Vote on other feature requests you'd like to see

Your feedback directly influences what we build next.
