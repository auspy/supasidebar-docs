SupaSidebar is a productivity sidebar for macOS that keeps your files, folders, and browser tabs organized in one place.

## Download & Install

### Option 1: Direct Download

1. Visit [supasidebar.com](https://www.supasidebar.com) and click **Download**.
2. Open the `.dmg` file and drag **SupaSidebar** into your **Applications** folder.
3. Launch SupaSidebar from Launchpad or Spotlight.

If you see a macOS security warning, go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Allow Anyway**.

### Option 2: Homebrew

```bash
brew install --cask supasidebar
```

After installing, SupaSidebar's guided onboarding walks you through permissions, sidebar placement, and feature selection based on your goals. You can also grant [Permissions](/gettingStarted/permissions) manually at any time.

- **Sidebar Panel** - Your central workspace with Recents, Favorites, and Spaces
- **Search Bar** - Find any saved item instantly with `Cmd Ctrl K`
- **Spaces** - Organize items into themed collections
- **Favorites** - Pin frequently accessed files and links
- **Recents** - Automatically tracks items from the past 48 hours
