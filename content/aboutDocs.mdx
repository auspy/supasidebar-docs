# About this documentation

A note on how these docs are written, and how to flag anything that's wrong.

## AI-assisted drafting

Some pages in this documentation are first drafted with AI assistance and then reviewed by the SupaSidebar team before publishing. This lets us cover more ground than we'd otherwise have time for - especially troubleshooting pages, mental-model explainers, and workflow guides.

AI gets the structure roughly right but occasionally invents details: setting names that don't exist, menu paths that look plausible but aren't real, or workflows that don't match the current build. We catch most of these in review, but some slip through.

## Found something wrong?

Email [admin@supasidebar.com](mailto:admin@supasidebar.com) with:

- The page URL where you spotted the issue
- What the page says
- What you actually see in the app (a screenshot helps)

We'll fix the page and reply when it's live. Replies usually go out within 1-2 hours during waking hours, 12 hours at the latest.

## What's always hand-verified

A few things on every page are checked against the running app, regardless of how the page was drafted:

- Keyboard shortcuts
- Menu paths (Preferences -> ...)
- Feature names
- Pricing claims

If any of those are wrong, that's especially worth flagging.
