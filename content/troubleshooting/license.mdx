# License issues

Most license problems are solved by a restart, or by freeing a device slot from the Manage License page on the web.

<Callout type="info">
License **deactivation is web-only**. There is no in-app deactivate button - all device management happens through the Manage License page on supasidebar.com. {/* VERIFY: confirm exact URL, e.g. supasidebar.com/manage */}
</Callout>

## Try a restart first

A quick restart clears most transient activation issues.

1. Quit SupaSidebar (right-click the menu bar icon -> Quit).
2. Reopen the app.

If the license is still flagged after a restart, continue below.

## Free a device slot from Manage License

Use this if you reinstalled SupaSidebar, replaced your Mac, or hit the device limit. You don't need access to the old Mac - everything happens in your browser.

1. Open the [Manage License](https://supasidebar.com) page on supasidebar.com. {/* VERIFY: exact URL and link copy */}
2. Sign in with the email you used at purchase.
3. Remove the device you no longer need.
4. On the Mac where you want to activate, open SupaSidebar and paste your license key when prompted.

{/* Screenshot: Manage License device list */}

## Moving to a new Mac

1. On the **new Mac**: install SupaSidebar from [supasidebar.com](https://supasidebar.com).
2. Paste your license key when prompted.
3. If activation fails because the old Mac is still using the slot, open [Manage License](https://supasidebar.com) and remove the old device. {/* VERIFY: exact URL */}

You don't need to do anything on the old Mac. License deactivation only happens on the web.

## Common activation errors

If activation fails, the error message usually points to one of these causes:

| Cause | What's happening | Fix |
|-------|------------------|-----|
| Device limit reached | This license has been activated on too many Macs. | Remove an old device from Manage License. |
| Invalid license key | The key has a typo or extra whitespace. | Copy-paste from your purchase email - don't retype. |
| Email not found | The email doesn't match any purchase record. | Check the inbox you used at purchase. BundleHunt customers see below. |
| License revoked | The license was disabled (refund, chargeback, or compromise). | Email `admin@supasidebar.com` if this is unexpected. |

## BundleHunt customers

If you bought SupaSidebar through BundleHunt, your license key is in your **BundleHunt order email**, not from SupaSidebar. Search your inbox for "supasidebar" or "BundleHunt order" to find the key. Use the email tied to your BundleHunt order on the Manage License page.

## Discount and promo codes

Licenses bought with a [discount code](/howTo/claimDiscount) behave exactly the same as full-price ones - same Manage License page, same device limit. The discount only affects the price.

## Still stuck?

Email `admin@supasidebar.com` with:

- The email you used at purchase
- The license key (or a screenshot of the error)
- The exact error message you're seeing

We reply within 1-2 hours during waking hours, and within 12 hours at the latest.
