# Privacy Blocklist

Keep certain sites or browsers out of [Live Tabs](/features/liveTabs) and [Recent Items](/features/recentItems) so your personal browsing stays private.

## How it works

The Privacy Blocklist filters what appears in Live Tabs and Recents. Any URL, domain, or entire browser you add to the blocklist is hidden from both surfaces. The blocklist only affects visibility in SupaSidebar - it does not block or alter your actual browsing.

## What you can block

| Type | Example | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| **URL** | `https://example.com/private` | Hides this specific page |
| **Domain** | `example.com` | Hides all pages from this domain |
| **Browser** | Safari | Hides all tabs and history from Safari |

## Setup

1. Open **Preferences → Privacy**
2. Click **Add to Blocklist**
3. Enter a URL, domain, or select a browser
4. The item is immediately hidden from Live Tabs and Recents

## Tips

- Blocking a browser hides all its tabs from [Live Tabs](/features/liveTabs) and all its history from [Recent Items](/features/recentItems)
- Domain blocking matches all subdomains - blocking `example.com` also hides `mail.example.com`
- Blocked items can still be saved to your sidebar manually - the blocklist only affects automatic tracking
