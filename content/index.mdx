# Welcome to Focusmo Documentation

Focusmo is the ultimate focus and productivity app designed to help you accomplish more with less distraction. Available for macOS and iOS, Focusmo provides powerful tools to manage your time, track your progress, and maintain deep focus.

## What is Focusmo?

Focusmo combines proven productivity techniques with modern technology to create a comprehensive focus management system. Whether you're a student, professional, or creative, Focusmo helps you:

- **Stay focused** with timer-based work sessions
- **Track progress** with detailed analytics
- **Manage tasks** with an intuitive task management system
- **Block distractions** with workspace automation (macOS)
- **Build habits** with consistent productivity routines
