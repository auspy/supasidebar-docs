## Introduction to SupaSidebar

SupaSidebar is a lightweight macOS menubar app that gives you instant access to your links, files, folders, and browser tabs — all from one place. Designed for speed and simplicity, it reduces app-switching, keeps your workflow organized, and helps you stay focused.




## What is SupaSidebar?

SupaSidebar is your **universal launcher** for macOS.

Save anything with a click, switch between browsers effortlessly, and open important resources in seconds.  
With global shortcuts, fast search, and customizable Spaces, SupaSidebar adapts to how *you* work — not the other way around.



## Who is SupaSidebar For?

SupaSidebar is built for anyone who works across multiple tools and wants a cleaner, more efficient workspace.

### **Professionals**
Organize essential files, folders, and project resources in one place. Reduce context switching and stay focused.

### **Designers & Developers**
Store assets, repos, docs, and tools in structured Spaces. Navigate instantly with global shortcuts and intelligent search.

### **Students & Researchers**
Collect notes, PDFs, and links into dedicated Spaces for each subject or project. Everything stays within quick reach.

### **Content Creators & Freelancers**
Keep media, drafts, templates, and client folders neatly organized. Streamline your creative workflow effortlessly.

### **Anyone Wanting a Unified Workspace**
If you constantly switch between Finder, browsers, and apps, SupaSidebar brings them all together in one clean, distraction-free sidebar.




## Key Benefits

### **Unified Access**
Access files, folders, links, and tabs from a single, consistent sidebar — no more juggling Finder windows or scattered bookmarks.

### **Smart Spaces**
Create dedicated Spaces for projects or workflows. Use Favorites for quick shortcuts and Recents to re-open items instantly.

### **Instant Search**
Open the global command (`CMD + CTRL + K`) to search across all your saved items and jump to anything in seconds.
