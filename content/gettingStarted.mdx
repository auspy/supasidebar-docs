# Installation

Welcome to SupaSidebar - your personal productivity assistant designed to keep your digital life neatly organized. This guide will help you install SupaSidebar efficiently on your Mac.

## Installation

Follow these steps to install SupaSidebar on macOS:

<br />
<p align="center">
  <img src="/assets/website.png" alt="Blurred background window" width="800" />
</p>

1. Visit the [official SupaSidebar website](https://www.supasidebar.com).  
2. Click **Download** and wait for the installation file to finish downloading.  
3. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file and drag **SupaSidebar** into your **Applications** folder.  
4. Launch SupaSidebar from **Launchpad** or **Spotlight Search**.  
5. Grant necessary permissions when prompted (Finder and Accessibility access) to enable quick shortcuts and file previews.
